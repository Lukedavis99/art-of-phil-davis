About the artist

Ex Jack of all trades, shopkeeper and even restaurant owner, I now spend all my time painting and teaching others how to paint.

Living by the coast in Ballycotton, Co. Cork and being based in my studio in Shanagarry, Co. Cork in Ireland, I enjoy endless inspiration around me. From beautiful landscape to daily fishing scenes and everything in between.

Classes

I currently hold classes everyday from Monday-Saturday for adults of all abilities between 11am and 1pm

Childrens classes are held Wednesday, Friday and Saturday between 3pm and 5 pm

Classes for groups for any type of occasion can also be catered for. For more information on classes, please use the "contact me" button.
